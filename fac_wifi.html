Wi-Fi-enabled Campus

Our College has a dedicated 20 MBPS Broad band connectivity which allows Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the campus. This facility allows students to access uninterrupted Internet connectivity anywhere within the campus. All the IT resources and content should be available to the faculty and students from their desk as well as anywhere-anytime basis over the campus. It is very helpful to work from any block/building and receive the same speed of data transfer.
