Science and Humanities:

The Department of Science and Humanities is constituted to inspire and promote the investigative spirit, as stepping stones for the overall intellectual and social development of students of technology and prepare them for the advance and technical endeavor of the undergraduate course.

This education intends to expand the students’ horizon of knowledge by exploring them to areas of study which sensitize their senses to a wide range of human problems and social phenomena. Such a holistic education would enable them to appreciate their role in nation’s reconstruction by responding to the challenges of the time.

Giving the Colour of Chemistry, with a force of Physics, imparting the brain of intellectual Mathematician, building a personality with Language, shaping in a definite structure, in a wonderful Environment, producing a Role Model into Society is Passion and Motive of our Department.

Facilities

The department of Science and Humanities provides the integrated courses required for acquiring good cornerstone in Basic science, so that they may have smooth transition from first year to second year and be ready to pursue advanced courses with the help of: English, Engineering Chemistry, Engineering Physics, Mathematics, Engineering Mechanics, C-programming, Environmental Science, Professional Ethics and Human Values, Engineering Drawing and Information Technology Workshop.

English Language Communication Skills, Advanced English Communication Skills, Chemistry and Physics laboratories are equipped with all the facilities, which is one of the unique assets of the Department.
