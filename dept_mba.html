Mission Statement
Our focus is not that you work towards “a piece of paper”, but rather you work towards achieving the knowledge, skills and confidence necessary to make ethical business decisions that not only increase the bottom-line, but also bring about positive change in the world. We create value for students and stakeholders by producing graduates who are prepared to compete successfully in a global context.  Along with the pursuit of discipline-based and practitioner based scholarship. Our faculty is also committed to teaching and nurturing students through a curriculum and pedagogy that emphasize experiential learning with broad access to teacher scholars.

Vision Statement

  • Faculty and staff work together to create and deliver relevant, high quality and innovative programs that serve our community.
  • Businesses and other organizations seek us out for qualified employees and for partnerships to meet their educational, consulting and service needs.
  • Our college is widely recognized for excellence and innovation in teaching, research and service.
  • Our college is also recognized as an inclusive place where students of opportunity can be successful.

Values Statement

         We value

  • Quality teaching, research, professional and public service by faculty and students.
  • Relevance and innovation in curriculum, instructional methods, research, professional and public service.
  • Intellectual growth, professional and ethical development of our students.
  • A collegial environment of academic freedom and faculty governance.
  • Diversity in our students, faculty, staff and community.
  • High standards of professional, ethical conduct; and a climate that fosters continuous improvement.
