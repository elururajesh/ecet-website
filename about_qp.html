VISION & MISSION

Our Vision
To be a world class knowledge enterprise striving for academic & professional excellence in technical education, research and development and service to the industry and society.


Objectives

  • To provide world class quality technical education in engineering disciplines.
  • To produce high quality, professionally well prepared graduates meeting the global standards with relevant social awareness.
  • To generate confidence, competitive and enterprising spirit among the students to face the Future challenges.
  • To extend the academic ambience to rural areas and to create entrepreneurship qualities and improve the living standards of their families, industry and society.
  • To nurture innovative and creative abilities of students and faculty and enhance quality of  human  resource with moral values
  • To Train the Students in self reliance, confidence, sense of personal responsibility of work.    
  • To Transfer the benefits of Science and Technology to the society.
  • To promote e-learning and e-governance in the institute.   

Our Mission
Ramachandra college  is committed to quality in teaching, research, public service.RCE maintains as its highest priority the education and development of its students in the  challenging environment of  present arena.


Our Mission is “To be a global institution that fosters elite education in science, technology, and other areas of scholarship that will best serve the nation and the world in the 21st century”.
